What is chattyhive?

Chattyhive is a social chat service that allows you to interact with other users through the topics you like and to create communities to share information in real time.

How does chattyhive work?

Users can subscribe to hives they like to follow or can create their own. A hive represents a topic or a community, which can be something as specific as an event in your city for the next weekend or something a lot more general like a popular game or a music genre.

Joining these hives users can connect, chat and share information with other users that have the same preferences, take part of debates, stay up to date about what’s happening on this topic, and make friends inside or outside the app thanks to geolocation and suggestions based on user’s activity.

How did the idea come about?

Even though the idea came up in 2010, it was kept in a drawer for a few years, and it wasn’t until September of 2013 when development actually started. It started as a personal need: I wanted a web service that would allow you to connect with new people chating about a topic you share, in the same way most human connections occur in real life, and I mostly could find websites that connects you to people by browsing profiles and photos instead of sharing a nice chat about a topic you have in common.

Now the project have evolved and its not only covering this main need I had in 2010, but it became a tool that will allow users to do much more: it will be a living community of communities and the users will be who decide how they can use it. We will just give them a flexible universe where they can share information in real time and organize it in hives, tags and categories.
